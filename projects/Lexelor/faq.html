  • What is Lexelor?

    Lexelor is a young startup with a very distinctive and responsible mission. We want to change the way advertising market works today. We believe that final users to whom the content is being showed must be a part of advertising paradigm. Users have power to the product, as what they offer is their service of watching the content to be produced.

  • What do we do?

    We allow any person with a smarthpone transform it into a source of solid income. We allow users to outsource unused smarthpone's time by watching ads. In simple words Lexelor gives you a key to monetizing your phone.

  • Who can use Lexelor?

    Anyone with a smartphone and acess to internet. We are working on making it even simpler, meaning that you would only need an internet connection once in a while, and rest of the time you won't even bother where you are and do you have a WiFi or data plan on. You will only need a credit card to checkout your earned money.

  • Where to start?

    To start making your first money with Lexelor all you need is to install our app and join the queue. If you feel uncomfortable or you do not know how to perform those two steps please check your guide we are sure it will help.

  • Will it effect my life?

    Yes and no. After you join Lexelor your life will definately change as additional income is never to worse. However, if you mean your day to day life than the answer is no. We tried to make is as seemless as possible. Our v.one makes it absolutely not intruding. Remember, we are here to change the way advertising business works.
